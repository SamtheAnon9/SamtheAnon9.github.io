Blogs

This page contains all of the blogs that I have contributed to. Blogs are arranged in order of when I contributed to them, with newer blogs higher up on this page.

twinstickmoba

twinstickmoba is a blog being produced while I am working on my end of year game for the second year of the Bachelor of Games Development and Virtual Worlds.

The game we are producing is 'Super Star Skirmish', a fast-paced mashup of the Twin-Stick Shooter and MOBA genres.

This blog describes the development of Super Star Skirmish (largely through screenshots and animated .gif files), and goes through the major events that happened along the way.

BAM!Code Powered Down Blog

The first blog that I have contributed to, this blog was produced while working on an assessment for the Bachelor of Games Development and Virtual Worlds.

The assessment required that I work with a group of other students to produce a game that could be run on multiple platforms- my group decided to develop a puzzle-platformer game about a broken-down robot, designed for the PC and Playstation Vita platforms.

This blog details the development of that game, 'Powered Down'.
