BAM!Code Powered Down Blog

The first (and, at the moment, only) blog that I have contributed to, this blog was produced while working on an assessment for the Bachelor of Games Development and Virtual Worlds.

The assessment required that I work with a group of other students to produce a game that could be run on multiple platforms- my group decided to develop a puzzle-platformer game about a broken-down robot, designed for the PC and Playstation Vita platforms.

This blog details the development of that game, 'Powered Down'.
